M1-DATA-REDIS-003 distributed scheduler lease summary

Implemented scope
- Production-only Redis-backed ShedLock configuration with namespace
  inner-cosmos-scheduler-v1; local development remains Redis-independent.
- Production startup fails closed when distributed scheduler leasing is disabled
  or its namespace is blank.
- Five business-side-effect schedules retain their existing cadence and behavior
  while gaining unique cross-pod leases:
  - letter-delivery: at most 5m, at least 55s
  - capsule-sync-retry: at most 5m, at least 55s
  - aurora-proactive: at most 10m, at least 85s
  - nightly-memory-settlement: at most 26h, at least 23h
  - session-idle-goodbye: at most 30m, at least 4m55s
- InMemoryRateLimitStore cleanup remains intentionally pod-local because it has
  no shared business side effect.

Verified behavior
- Two independent Redis lock providers cannot hold the same lease concurrently.
- Releasing a lease permits another pod to acquire it.
- An abandoned lease expires and another pod recovers execution.
- The real Spring ShedLock advisor skips a concurrent annotated invocation.
- Redis connection failure propagates instead of running a leased job fail-open.
- Production TLS smoke observed four live scheduler lease keys in Redis.

Product-semantics boundary
- No Aurora proactive decision logic, Self/Constitution/Emergence state,
  personality/relationship evolution, user portrait, capsule dialogue, matching,
  starfield, slow-letter, or Goodbye semantics were removed or narrowed.
- This change coordinates duplicate execution only; it does not reduce proactive
  budgets, message capability, scheduling cadence, or user-visible innovation.

Known limitation
- ShedLock's classical Redis provider is an at-most-once coordination mechanism;
  Redis master failover guarantees require later infrastructure resilience work.
- Short-lived Aurora/stream state and resumable Redis Stream relay remain pending.
- Provider-specific distributed quota coordination remains pending where required.
- Independent security review remains pending.
- DATA-REDIS remains IN_PROGRESS; this is not whole-product completion evidence.
